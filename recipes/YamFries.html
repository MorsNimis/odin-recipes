Yam Fries recipe

Description

This recipe will result in a nice crispy yet healthy alternative to regular french fries. Circular cut yam fries are one of my favourite snacks to have when I'm craving something starchy.

Ingredients

  • Yams
  • Olive oil
  • Your choice of seasoning salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Cut the yams into thin circles length wise
  3. Get a baking sheet and lay tin foil over top
  4. Lightly spread olive oil over the tin foil
  5. Place the now sliced yams evenly spaced on the baking sheet
  6. Lightly drizzle olive oil over the yams
  7. Sprinkle seasoning on yams
  8. Place in oven for 20-25 minutes
  9. Enjoy!
